Nutrition Tips

  • Eat antioxidant-rich foods like berries, spinach, and nuts
  • Include omega-3 fatty acids from fish or flaxseeds
  • Reduce sugar intake to prevent collagen breakdown
  • Vitamin C from citrus fruits helps collagen production
  • Stay hydrated with water-rich fruits and vegetables

Sleep & Rest Tips

  • Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night
  • Use silk or satin pillowcases to reduce friction
  • Establish a consistent sleep schedule
  • Avoid screens 1 hour before bedtime
  • Practice relaxation techniques before bed

Hydration Tips

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily
  • Include herbal teas for added hydration
  • Eat water-rich foods like cucumber and watermelon
  • Use a humidifier in dry environments
  • Avoid excessive caffeine and alcohol

Stress Management

  • Practice daily meditation or deep breathing
  • Take regular breaks during work
  • Connect with friends and family regularly
  • Engage in hobbies that bring you joy
  • Consider yoga or tai chi for stress relief

Exercise Tips

  • Aim for 30 minutes of moderate exercise daily
  • Include both cardio and strength training
  • Don't forget to stretch before and after workouts
  • Exercise outdoors for vitamin D when possible
  • Cleanse skin after sweating to prevent breakouts
